A world setting created by Jacob Hitchcock, Nikolas Pendley, Hunter Rhodes, and Dalton Morrow.

The collection of planes; including Atost, Dhaer Shesh, Arkadia, and Mare'Lora; is known as Jandoryn.

The Progenitors named the system Jandoryn, a word meaning ‘Fourfold Design’, a reference to the four architects who shaped its laws.

Atost

Atost is made up of four main continents, the biggest of which is Patriem. Patriem has been the setting for some of Atost's most significant events across all of history. Patriem would come to be scarred by the greatest of the Maravar, Mobier the Druid.

Mare'Lora

Mare 'Lora was created in full by nine deities wielding ancient magic known as the songs of creation, but this magic had a price for as each deity finished the song they were cast into a deep slumber, hidden throughout the world that they crafted as their own “paradise”. These deities would then be known as The Azae throughout Mare ’Lora.

Arkadia

Welcome to the world of Arcadia, long ago in the distant past there was a cataclysmic upheaval the cause of which is unknown to its many inhabitants. But that's all in the past now Arcadia is a bustling world of commerce, adventure, and pirates.
