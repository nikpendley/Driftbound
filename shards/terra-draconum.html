Terra Draconum

The Lands of the Scale

Terra Draconum is known as the Lands of the Scale, a shard defined by draconic presence, ancient power, and the weight of creatures who shape the land around them.

Dragons are central to the identity of this shard, making Terra Draconum one of Mare’Lora’s most imposing and mythic regions.

Draconic Presence

Terra Draconum is a shard where dragons are not a distant rumor. Their presence shapes the land, its dangers, its politics, and the way outsiders speak of the region.
