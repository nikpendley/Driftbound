Ondreier

The Lands of Deception

Run-Ath-Ik, known more commonly as Ondreier, is the Lands of Deception. Its identity is bound to misdirection, hidden motives, and the dangerous beauty of things that are not what they appear to be.

Ondreier is also tied to Saryn Hinual’u, awakener of Wylint’ess Aul Nelluon, steward of Cerlyn Gyrah and the Feather of the Raven.

Saryn Hinual’u

Saryn Hinual’u is the awakener of Wylint’ess Aul Nelluon, who woke the Azea from slumber after creating the shard of Mare'Lora.

Wylint’ess Aul Nelluon

Wylint’ess Aul Nelluon is the Azae of Shard 4 and steward of Cerlyn Gyrah, called the Feather of the Raven of the House of the Raven.
