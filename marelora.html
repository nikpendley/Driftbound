Mare'Lora

Mare 'Lora was created in full by these nine deities wielding ancient magic known as the songs of creation but this magic had a price.

Each deity finished the song, but they were cast into a deep slumber, hidden throughout the world that they crafted as their own “paradise”. These deities would then be known as The Azae, throughout Mare ’Lora.

Shard 1 - Flamel

Flamel, otherwise known as The Lands of Burgeoning, was largely peaceful up until The Godfall. When the gods fell they were scattered across Atost and Mare 'Lora.

The Azae of Flamel supposedly fell from the heavens and landed on the peak of Mare ’ Loras’ largest mountain, and in the process fractured the land, splitting Mare 'Lora into what would be later referred to as the Shards of Song.
