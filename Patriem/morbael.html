Morbael

The Untamed Far North

Morbael is the ancient land of the far north, largely untouched by the greater world.

Its people are practical, vicious, and hardy, scattered across hundreds of villages in the unforgiving tundra.

They struggle to survive, offer prayers to the spirits of the land, and often feud over resources and habitable territory.

Overview

Morbael is not a single city or kingdom, but a harsh northern expanse of villages, spirits, feuds, and survival. Its people are shaped by the tundra, and the tundra does not tolerate weakness.

Spirits of the Land

The people of Morbael offer prayers to the spirits that inhabit the land. These spirits are not distant symbols, but immediate presences tied to survival, weather, hunting, and the deadly balance of the far north.

Thornblades

Rumors speak of powerful weapons called Thornblades, forged from the souls of many dead. Whether they are sacred relics, cursed arms, or both depends on which village tells the tale.
