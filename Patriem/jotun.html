Jotun

The Industrial City of War

Jotun is the most modern city of the north, powered by industry and zealous religion.

Its people follow the Church of War, remnants of the touch of a now-dead god, and seek to expand their control across the entirety of the north.

This ambition brings them into conflict with Morbael and the greater northern lands.

Overview

Jotun is a city of smoke, steel, faith, and conquest. Its industry has pushed it beyond many of its neighbors technologically, while its religious zeal has made it one of the most aggressive powers in northern Patriem.

The Church of War

The Church of War is the spiritual engine of Jotun. Born from the remnants of a dead god’s influence, it teaches expansion, strength, and dominance as sacred obligations.

Northern Ambition

Jotun’s desire to control the north has placed it at odds with Morbael and the scattered peoples of the tundra. To Jotun, the north is a destiny waiting to be claimed; to Morbael, it is a homeland that refuses to kneel.
