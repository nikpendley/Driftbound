Northwest Mountains

A sealed dwarven enclave locked away for thousands of years.

The Northwest Mountains hold a sealed dwarven enclave that has remained locked for thousands of years. Little is known of what survives within, whether ancient dwarven power, forgotten treasure, old shame, or something better left buried. For explorers and historians, the sealed enclave is one of Patriem’s great unanswered questions. There have been rumors of dwarves emerging from the mountains in the last 100 years, and some have even reintegrated into modern society, though they refuse to speak of the settlements in the NW Mountains.
