The Divide

A central route through Patriem, raised by Mobier and used to avoid the Tiefling Lands.

The Divide is one of Patriem’s most important geographic features, raised by Mobier and remembered as a scar across the continent.

It serves as a route to the south for travelers seeking to avoid the danger of the Tiefling Lands.

At its center is a gate, making the Divide not only a passage but a strategic and symbolic landmark in Patriem’s history.
