Folstead

Capital of the Heartlands

Folstead is the recently emancipated capital city of the Heartlands.

It is governed by High Lord Derrick, who led the war to free the region from the tyrannical rule of Lord Drakes.

Overview

Folstead is a city still learning what freedom means. Its streets carry the memory of tyranny, rebellion, and the hard work of rebuilding a region after liberation.

High Lord Derrick

High Lord Derrick governs Folstead after leading the war that freed the Heartlands. To many, he is a liberator; to others, he is the man now responsible for ensuring that freedom survives the peace.

The Heartlands

As the capital of the Heartlands, Folstead is becoming the center of southern Patriem’s new political identity. Its future will shape whether the region becomes a stable power or falls back into conflict.
