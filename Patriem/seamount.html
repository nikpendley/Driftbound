Seamount

The Jewel of the Emerald Coast

Seamount is a major trade city in northern Patriem, known for agriculture, artifacts, and the event that helped give rise to the Slayer’s Guild.

The city now serves as one of the continent’s great hubs, drawing merchants, guild agents, relic hunters, and travelers from across Atost.

Its wealth and influence make Seamount one of the most important northern powers in Patriem.

Many mobs and criminal empires are allowed to operate and rule throughout the city.

The City is broken up into districts known as Gates: Sea, Snow, Sand, and Mountain.

Sea Gate

The Great Port sees thousands of ships cross it hundreds of docks ferrying cargo and passengers to the south, north and far beyond Patriems shores.

Great coral reefs span the coastline glimmering in many colors just under the water giving quite the sight to travelers visiting to see the fabled Emerald Coast.

Snow Gate

Snow gate sees primary trade with the northern lands with a train network, moving metals and other mined resources and goods from Jotun.

Much of the cities industrial infrastructure resides here.

Sand Gate

For many a traveler, the first sight of Seamount comes from those first few steps through the Sand Gate.

First gazing upon the Great Mountain ringed with clouds brought from the sea, then the Emerald coast yawning to the Unending Horizon.

Many people make their homes and businesses here, serving not just the Greater Trades but the whole city with many services.

Mountain Gate

Home to Seamounts Nobility and Bureaucracy, many of the city's banks and civil infrastructure resides here.

Acting as the beating heart of the city, Mountain Hold pumps the city's glittering lifeblood: GOLD.

The base of the mountain holds many high-end businesses, while further up the mountain reside the extravagant villas and homes of the affluent.

Near the middle heights of the mountain lies The Castle of King Jeric Melados, who has ruled far beyond what his human lifespan should permit.
