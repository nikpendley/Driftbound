The Moonlands

The Elven Glades of the Marsh

The Moonlands lie in the far northwest of Patriem and are home to elusive elven peoples who are rarely seen by outsiders. The glades are quiet, old, and difficult to fully map, protected as much by mystery as by wilderness. Travelers often speak of the Elven Glades as if they are half-real: a place known on maps, but rarely confirmed by those who enter.
