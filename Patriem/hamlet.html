Hamlet

The City of Light

Hamlet is a small island in the center of the northern lake.

It is home to the Grand Church of Light, which worships the god of the Sun, the Bright One.

Hamlet is small in size but immense in spiritual significance. Resting in the center of the northern lake, the island serves as one of the great religious centers of Patriem.

The Grand Church of Light

The Grand Church of Light worships the Bright One, god of the Sun. Pilgrims, priests, and faithful travelers come to Hamlet seeking guidance, blessing, and the warmth of divine light.
