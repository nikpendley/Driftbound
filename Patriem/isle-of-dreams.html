The Isle of Dreams

Uncharted Isles of the Western Sea

Hundreds of unexplored islands make up the Isle of Dreams, a scattered chain of mystery, danger, and opportunity off the greater west coast of Patriem.

Foqal, Lord of the Seas, patrols the waters around the islands and the western coastline, making the region both alluring and treacherous for explorers, merchants, and would-be settlers.

The Isle of Dreams remains one of Patriem’s most untamed frontiers. Its islands are largely unmapped, and many who sail among them return with stories of hidden coves, strange ruins, impossible treasures, and dangers that refuse to stay buried.

Foqal, Lord of the Seas

Foqal is the dominant power on the waters surrounding the Isle of Dreams. Whether seen as a tyrant, protector, criminal king, or necessary evil depends entirely on who is asked and how recently their ship passed through his waters.

He sails with the aid of a sailbeast by the name of No’tholegra.
