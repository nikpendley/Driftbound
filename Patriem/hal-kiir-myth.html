Hal-Kiir Myth

The Pale Jeweled City

Hal-Kiir Myth translates into the common tongue as The Pale Jeweled City.

Hidden deep within the Great Dragon’s Breath Desert, the city has a notably high density of magic users and is ruled by a council of five Magi known as the Archmagi.

Its people are wary of outsiders, raising the question of whether Hal-Kiir Myth will one day join the greater world politically or maintain its wall of separation.

Overview

Hal-Kiir Myth is one of Patriem’s most mysterious hidden cities. Surrounded by desert and guarded by secrecy, it has developed apart from the greater political world.

The Archmagi

The city is ruled by five powerful Magi known as the Archmagi. In Hal-Kiir Myth, the word Magi refers to casters, whose presence is far more common here than in most known populations.

Possible Maravar Bloodlines

The people appear primarily human, but their unusually high number of natural-born casters has led some to theorize that they descend from an ancient sub-sect of Maravar.

Firearms and Isolation

Hal-Kiir Myth maintains a military force that uses firearms not dissimilar from wetlock technology found in the land of Mobeir. This makes the city both isolated and dangerous to underestimate.
