Ancient Ruins

The remains of a powerful ancient civilization, now dangerous, violent, and largely lost to time.

The Ancient Ruins were once the seat of a powerful civilization tied to Mobier, the great and legendary druid who raised the Divide.

Over time, the ruins were taken over by Tieflings and became one of the more violent and dangerous areas of Patriem.

Beasts, demons, and other threats haunt the region, but the ruins remain bountiful for explorers seeking lost knowledge, ancient artifacts, and pieces of Patriem’s blank history.
