Tyrrain

The Northern Darkin Stronghold

Tyrrain is a northern region with a large Darkin-heavy population, founded by the Shaded Mage.

The region is well provided for and generally content, though it remains extremely exclusive about who is allowed to live in or around the city.

Tyrrain is one of the most secure and self-contained regions of Patriem. Its people value privacy, protection, and cultural preservation, making the region difficult for outsiders to enter and even harder for them to understand.

Legacy of the Shaded Mage

Founded by the Shaded Mage, Tyrrain carries an old and powerful legacy. Its Darkin population views that inheritance not merely as history, but as a responsibility to be guarded from the influence of the wider continent.
