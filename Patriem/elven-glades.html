Elven Glades

The far western glades of Patriem, where elves are rarely seen and rarely understood.

The Elven Glades lie in the far west of Patriem and are home to elusive elven peoples who are rarely seen by outsiders.

The glades are quiet, old, and difficult to fully map, protected as much by mystery as by wilderness.

Travelers often speak of the Elven Glades as if they are half-real: a place known on maps, but rarely confirmed by those who enter.
