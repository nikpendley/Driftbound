The Grand Arcanum

Atost’s Most Significant School of Magic

The Grand Arcanum is Atost’s most significant school of magic, having served as home to some of the most brilliant magical minds across Jandoryn.

The school is divided into five major houses: the Phoenix, the Firestags, the Owlbears, the Dragons, and the Ravens. Each house carries its own identity, values, and magical reputation.

The Five Houses

Phoenix

The House of the Phoenix is known for bright, passionate perfection. Its students often pursue brilliance, renewal, and the highest standard of magical mastery.

Owlbear

The House of the Owlbear values practicality, heartiness, and strength. Its members stand together as a pact, relying on endurance, loyalty, and collective resilience.

Firestags

The House of the Firestags burns heartily, carrying a spirit of courage, warmth, ambition, and relentless magical drive.

Dragons

The House of the Dragons is proud and fierce. Its students are often marked by confidence, force of will, and a commanding presence within the arcane world.

Ravens

The House of the Ravens are masters of mystery, the unknown, and the delve. They seek hidden truths, forgotten lore, and the secrets buried beneath magic itself.

Faculty of the Grand Arcanum

Headmaster Ezekiel Reign

Headmaster Ezekiel Reign serves as the leading authority of the Grand Arcanum. He is associated with an Owlbear pet named Bosco.

Librarian Cromwell Snotblight

Cromwell Snotblight is a hill giant scholar of rhetoric, abjuration, and alteration. Known for his intelligence, eloquence, and gentle nature, Cromwell once taught protective spells and wards in a large auditorium built to accommodate his size. Though once considered likely to become headmaster, he has since retired into the role of school librarian.

Illusionist Professor Pelendo Rehtost

Pelendo Rehtost is a silver Dragonborn teacher of illusions, famous for playing elaborate tricks on first-year students. Despite his joking nature, he is an incredibly gifted illusionist, capable of maintaining complex illusions across the entire campus. He created the spell Pelendo’s Conundrum, which turns the user invisible, teleports them to safety, and leaves three mirror images in their place.

Enchanter Professor Bastard SliverFlow

Bastard SliverFlow is a rare dwarven enchantment mage with a fiery temper. First-year students quickly learn his rule: “If I can break it with my hands, start over.” Ironically, he wears magical gloves designed for exactly that purpose.

Transmutationist Professor Wind

Wind Ever Blowing, Leaves Ever Falling, Life Ever Changing — known simply as Wind — is a female transmutationist teacher of the Grand Arcanum.

Evocationist Professor Min

Minimus Maximus, the Great Lord of Fire and All that Burns in All His Magnitude — known as Professor Min — teaches evocation at the Grand Arcanum.

Abjuration Professor Petricor Samble

Petricor Samble serves as an abjuration professor at the Grand Arcanum.

Conjuration Professor Jame Tolk V

Jame Tolk V teaches conjuration and is believed to be a descendant of the original Jame Tolk, though this remains assumed rather than confirmed.

Divination Professor Calliope Vey

Calliope Vey teaches divination at the Grand Arcanum.
