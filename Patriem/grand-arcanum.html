The Grand Arcanum

Atost’s Most Significant School of Magic

The Grand Arcanum is Atost’s most significant school of magic, having served as home to some of the most brilliant magical minds across Jandoryn.

The school is divided into five major houses: the Phoenix, the Firestags, the Owlbears, the Dragons, and the Ravens. Each house carries its own identity, values, and magical reputation.

Phoenix

The House of the Phoenix is known for bright, passionate perfection. Its students are often driven by ideals of brilliance, renewal, and mastery.

Owlbear

The House of the Owlbear values practicality, heartiness, and strength. Its members are bound together as a pact, relying on endurance, loyalty, and collective resilience.

Firestags

The House of the Firestags is known for burning heartily, carrying a spirit of courage, warmth, and tireless magical ambition.

Dragons

The House of the Dragons is proud and fierce. Its students are often marked by confidence, force of will, and a commanding presence within the arcane world.

Ravens

The House of the Ravens are masters of mystery, the unknown, and the delve. They seek hidden truths, forgotten lore, and the secrets buried beneath the surface of magic itself.
