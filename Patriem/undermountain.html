Undermountain

A great lawful city beneath the mountains, home to kobolds, goblins, and gnolls.

Undermountain is one of Patriem’s great dark-dwelling cities, a lawful subterranean realm built beneath the southern mountains.

Its population includes kobolds, goblins, and gnolls, who have carved out a structured civilization away from the surface world.

To outsiders, Undermountain can seem dangerous or strange, but within its depths is a society with its own laws, customs, and internal order.
