The Dragon's Breath Desert

The remains of a powerful ancient civilization, now dangerous, violent, and largely lost to time.

The The Dragon's Breath Desert is ancient ruins of what was once the seat of a powerful civilization tied to Mobier, the great and legendary Maravar who raised the Divide. Over time, the desert was taken over by criminals and became one of the more violent and dangerous areas of Patriem. Beasts, demons, and other threats haunt the region, but the desert remains bountiful for explorers seeking lost knowledge, ancient artifacts, and pieces of Patriem’s blank history.
