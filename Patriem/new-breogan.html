New Breogan

The Ogen Refuge in the Western Mountains

New Breogan was founded after the tragic loss of the Ogen homelands.

Overview

New Breogan is a place of grief, survival, and chosen isolation. Built by the Ogen after the loss of their homeland, it stands as both refuge and reminder.

The Ogen

The Ogen withdrew from the wider peoples of Patriem, seeking safety and identity in the lower western mountains. Their seclusion is not simply distrust; it is the result of a wound that has not yet healed.
