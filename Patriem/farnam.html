Farnam

An Orc, Human, and Ironborn village of the Mid-South ruled by Kaine.

Farnam is an Orc, Human, and Ironborn village of Patriem’s Mid-South that sees trade from across the continent.

Ruled by King Conrad, Farnam carries a history rich with death, redemption, and political weight.

Despite its village identity, Farnam belongs to one of the most powerful civilizations in Atost and stands as an important center of culture, memory, and trade.
