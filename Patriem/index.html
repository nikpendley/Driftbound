Patriem

A vast continent of trade, hidden kingdoms, ancient ruins, and dangerous routes shaped by the legacy of Mobier.

Patriem is one of the great continents of Atost, vast in scale and diverse in culture, terrain, and danger.

From the trade city of Seamount in the north to the lawful dark-dwelling kingdoms beneath Undermountain, Patriem is a continent defined by movement, secrecy, old wounds, and opportunity.

Much of its history has been lost, buried, or deliberately left blank. Some places are thriving centers of trade, while others are sealed away, swallowed by nature, or claimed by violent powers.
