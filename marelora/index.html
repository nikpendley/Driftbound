Mare'Lora

A world shaped by the Songs of Creation, the sleeping Azae, and the nine Shards of Song.

Mare’Lora was created by nine deities wielding ancient magic known as the Songs of Creation, but that magic demanded a price.

Each deity finished the song and was cast into a deep slumber, hidden throughout the paradise they had crafted. These sleeping deities became known throughout Mare’Lora as the Azae.

When the gods fell, the world was fractured into the nine Shards of Song. Each shard now carries its own identity, mystery, and legacy.

  • Shard 1 - Flamel
  • Shard 2 - Terra Draconum
  • Shard 3 - Run-Ath-Tel
  • Shard 4 - Ondreier
  • Shard 5 - Run-Ath-Menartu
  • Shard 6 - Furum
  • Shard 7 - Tersyio
  • Shard 8 - Theatrum Mentis
  • Shard 9 - Hammerfall
