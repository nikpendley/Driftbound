Flamel

The Lands of Burgeoning

Flamel, otherwise known as the Lands of Burgeoning, was largely peaceful until the Godfall. When the gods fell, they were scattered across Atost and Mare’Lora.

The Azae of Flamel supposedly fell from the heavens and landed on the peak of Mare’Lora’s largest mountain. The impact fractured the land, splitting Mare’Lora into what would later be called the Shards of Song.

The Godfall and the Fracturing

Flamel is remembered as the place where the fall of the Azae changed Mare’Lora forever. The impact on the world’s greatest mountain did not merely scar the land; it divided the world into the Shards of Song.
